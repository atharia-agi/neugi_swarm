Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

NEUGI is a fully local, open-source agentic framework. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data to external servers. All memory, conversations, and configuration remain on your local machine.

Third-Party Services

When you configure external LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.), your data is sent directly to those providers according to their respective privacy policies. NEUGI does not act as a intermediary.

Local Storage

NEUGI stores data locally in SQLite databases and JSON configuration files within your designated NEUGI directory. You have full control over this data.
